Best practices and tips

To ensure your code scans perfectly every time, keep these tips in mind:

Check the Contrast

Always use a dark color for the QR pattern and a light color for the background. Dark black on white is the safest option.

Size Matters

If printing on a small item like a business card, ensure the code is at least 2cm x 2cm (0.8 inches).

Test Before Printing

Always scan the code with at least two different smartphone models (e.g., an iPhone and an Android) before ordering a large print run.

Mind the Placement

Do not place the QR code in the crease of a flyer or near the edge where it might get cut off.
